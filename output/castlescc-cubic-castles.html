Castles.cc (Cubic Castles)

Castles.CC (Cubic Castles) is an open world MMO game where you can build, trade, mine, craft, and roleplay! Create your dream world, fight battles, and explore with other players in the active community.

Cubic Castles Features

Customize your character

Create the ideal character with a variety of styles to suit you. Shop for hats, shirts, pants, wings, and many other accessories in the Cubit store.

Build your own world

In Castles.CC you can shape the terrain and build your own world. In that world of your own, you can mine resources, craft items, create a farm, and much more.

Explore vast landscapes

Explore and mine various lands. Forests, deserts, mountain ranges, arctic landscapes, tropical islands - all open for adventure with resources unique to that biome.

Race and fight

Castles.CC isn’t just about building and exploring. There’s plenty of action too. You can drive cars freely for fun or compete in races. You can also fight other players in PvP wand battles!
